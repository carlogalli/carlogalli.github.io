Carlo Galli

Welcome!

I am an Assistant Professor of Economics at Universidad Carlos III de Madrid, and a Research Affiliate at CEPR in the MEF and IMF programs.

I obtained a PhD in Economics from UCL in 2020. I was a Postdoctoral Researcher under the Chair in Sovereign Debt at SciencesPo during the 2020-21 academic year.

My research interests are in macroeconomics, international finance and information economics. My work studies fiscal and monetary policy, with a particular focus on sovereign debt crises and default risk.
